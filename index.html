Project 1: Time-Resolved Photoluminescence with CW Lasers

In this project, I am exploring the dynamics of electron-hole recombination using Continuous Wave (CW) lasers. By analyzing the decay of light emission, we can better understand the quantum efficiency of semiconductor materials. This project was started this semester and focuses on the intersection of optical physics and material science.
